Bitcoin (BTC)
Bitcoin is the first decentralized digital currency, created in 2009. It operates on a peer-to-peer network and allows for secure and instant transfer of value without the need for intermediaries.
Ethereum (ETH)
Ethereum is a blockchain-based platform that enables the creation of decentralized applications and smart contracts.
Ripple (XRP)
Ripple is a digital currency designed for fast and secure cross-border transactions.
Cardano (ADA)
Cardano is a blockchain-based platform that aims to deliver a more secure and sustainable ecosystem for the development and execution of smart contracts and decentralized applications.
Dogecoin (DOGE)
Dogecoin started as a joke in 2013, inspired by the popular "Doge" meme. Despite its origins, it has since become a widely-used cryptocurrency and has established a strong community of supporters and developers.
Binance Coin (BNB)
Binance Coin can be used to pay for trading fees on the exchange and offers various discounts and benefits for users. In addition to its utility on the Binance platform, Binance Coin has also gained popularity as a standalone digital asset.
